Chapter One

Our Story

How two people became one story — the short version.

Spring 2019

How We Met

A mutual friend's birthday dinner, a table for twelve, and the only two empty seats left — right next to each other. James blamed it on fate; Eleanor blamed it on bad seating charts. Either way, neither of us left early.

Winter 2020

The First Trip

A long weekend upstate turned into a running joke about who packed better, who got them lost on the trail, and who insisted on stopping at every diner along the way. We still argue about the directions.

Summer 2023

Moving In

Two apartments became one, two coffee orders became a shared routine, and somewhere between unpacking boxes and arguing over shelf space, it started to feel like home.

Fall 2025

The Proposal

A quiet evening, a favorite trail, and a question that didn't need much of an answer. It was always going to be yes.
