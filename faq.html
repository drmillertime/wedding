Chapter Six

Frequently Asked

If your question isn't here, just reach out — we're happy to help.

What should I wear?

Garden formal. Soft neutrals and earth tones look lovely against the setting — flat or block-heel shoes are recommended since the ceremony is on grass.

Are kids welcome?

We love your little ones, but we've decided to keep the celebration adults-only so everyone can relax and enjoy the evening.

Can I bring a plus-one?

We've kept our guest list intentionally close. Your invitation will note if a plus-one is included — if you're unsure, just ask.

Is the venue indoor or outdoor?

Both — the ceremony and cocktail hour are outdoors, with dinner and dancing in a covered, open-air barn.

Will there be transportation?

A shuttle will run between the recommended hotels and the venue in the evening — details to follow closer to the date.

What's the RSVP deadline?

We'd love to hear from you by August 1, 2026 — you can RSVP right here on the site.
